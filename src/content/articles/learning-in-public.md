There's a temptation to wait until something is polished before sharing it. I'm
trying to resist that.

## The case for sharing early

- **Feedback is cheaper before you've over-invested.** A rough draft invites
  course corrections; a finished piece invites defensiveness.
- **It creates a paper trail.** Future-me can see how past-me thought about a
  problem.
- **It lowers the stakes.** Not every post has to be definitive. Some are just
  notes.

> The best time to write something down is right after you understood it, while
> it still feels surprising.

So that's the plan for this site: publish often, keep it honest, and edit later
if I'm wrong.

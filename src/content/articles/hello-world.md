Welcome to my corner of the internet. This is where I'll publish **articles**
about things I'm learning and document the **projects** I'm building.

## Why a blog?

Writing forces me to actually understand a topic well enough to explain it.
Plus, it's nice to have a permanent home for ideas instead of letting them
disappear into chat logs.

## How this works

Every article is just a Markdown file in `src/content/articles/`. To publish a
new one, I add a file with some frontmatter at the top:

```yaml
---
title: 'My new post'
description: 'A short summary.'
pubDate: 2026-06-16
tags: ['example']
---
```

...then write the body in Markdown. That's it — commit, push, done.

Thanks for reading!
